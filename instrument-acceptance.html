Manufacturer Information

To be accepted for sale, each instrument must include complete manufacture details. These fields help establish provenance and support the instrument's authenticity.

  • Manufacturer name
  • Manufacturer address
  • Date of manufacture

Ownership History

The store requires a documented chain of ownership for all instruments. This information improves buyer confidence and reinforces the product's trustworthiness.

  • Date and location of each owner
  • Supporting documents for each transfer
  • Reason for transfer of ownership
  • Price paid by each new owner
  • Proof of ownership documents

Seller Agreement Requirements

Sellers must agree to the store's inspection and transfer terms before ownership changes hands.

  • The seller must provide written agreement allowing the item to be inspected before transfer of ownership.
  • If the item is deemed unacceptable after inspection, the current owner agrees to pay the cost of the inspection.

Why These Requirements Matter

These criteria support the website's mission of delivering rare instruments with strong provenance and trusted restoration services. Clear documentation helps preserve historical value and ensures legitimate ownership for buyers and sellers.
