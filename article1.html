Lab Notes: Blue Horizons

In this feature, we examine the impact of blue shades on innovation spaces and editorial tone. Blue is a calming yet energetic foundation that helps creative teams focus, while white space gives every story room to breathe. From experimental photography to design systems, the colour palette becomes a discreet collaborator.

The article highlights case studies from editorial workshops where blue was used to steer attention and create a sense of trust. We also compare how blue accents in layout and photography can balance dense information with visual calm.

Readers will find practical takeaways for using blue in cover design, feature spreads, and digital presentations. The story concludes with a reflection on how colour can be a quiet but powerful editorial partner.
