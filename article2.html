The Sound of Science

This feature explores the intersection of acoustics, ambient awareness, and the scientific workspace. We trace the role of sound design in laboratories, from quiet concentration to experimental data sonification.

We interview designers who create acoustic environments for experimental teams and describe how sound can guide attention, reveal hidden patterns, and make data feel more human.

The story offers practical examples of using audio cues in project workflows without distracting from the deep work that drives innovation.
